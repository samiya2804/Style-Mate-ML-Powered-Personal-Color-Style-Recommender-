Empowering Your Style, Scientifically

StyleMate bridges the gap between digital colorimetry and personal fashion consultation.

Our Mission

We believe finding your perfect color palette should be accessible, accurate, and fun. StyleMate was founded on the principle of combining classic color analysis theory (like the 4-season method) with modern computer vision technology (CIELAB color space analysis) to deliver highly personalized style recommendations.

Technology Under the Hood

Our analysis relies on reliable, industry-standard metrics to determine your true undertone and contrast level.

  • CIELAB Color Space
  • Delta-E Metrics
  • Personalized Swatch Selection
  • Seasonal Pattern Matching

Our Commitment to You

Your privacy is paramount. We do not store or share uploaded photos. Our goal is to provide you with the confidence and knowledge to make brilliant wardrobe and makeup choices, ensuring you always look your most vibrant self.
